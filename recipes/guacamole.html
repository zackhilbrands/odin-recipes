Gaucamole

Guacamolo is an avocado based dip that originated in the country of Mexico. The avocado gives the dip it's signature green color.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peel 'em, pit 'em, and mash 'em
  • 1 lime, juice it!
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
